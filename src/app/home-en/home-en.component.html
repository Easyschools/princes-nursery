About us

who we are

PRINCE and QUEEN Nursery, founded in 1989, is a part of the educational project of Oasis School Group which consists of three sections:
-Prince and Queen Nursery
-Oasis international school, the first school in the world authorized to teach in French the 4 IB programs (International baccalaureate – www.ibo.org
-and Oasis de Maadi, our national curriculum school.

The methods used in PRINCE and QUEEN Nursery are the same as of Oasis Kindergarten’s: children are well prepared to integrate to the upper stages of school through early language learning (French or English in addition to Arabic) and many activities which stimulates children’s growth, and encourages to build their personality and skills.

We pay particular importance to the discovery of books to develop their interest in reading. We also integrate the latest technologies in education, such as smart boards, laptops and Ipad to allow interactive teaching.

PRINCE and QUEEN Nursery creates a positive and protective environment for babies and young children from 6 months to 4 years, so they can discover, communicate, and learn to express themselves in a stimulating and secure environment in classes supervised by a trained teacher and a teacher’s assistant.

Mission & Vision

PRINCE and QUEEN Nursery is a big family. Simply cross the threshold of the nursery to feel the family atmosphere that is essential to the development of our children.

“Excellence and Fulfillment” is our motto. Our trained and experienced teachers invest to provide children with personalized education tailored to each profile.

Admission

  1. Book an appointment from 9 am to 3 pm
  2. Admission process will be as follows:
    • Fill the “Request for admission” form at the premises of the nursery
    • Set the starting day and time
    • Receive an email with application and the regulations of the nursery. Fill in the application and send it back (before your child’s first day)
  3. Requested documents are:
    • Computerized copy of the birth certificate of the child
    • Three recent identity pictures
    • Copy of the certificate of vaccination
    • Copy of both parents ID
